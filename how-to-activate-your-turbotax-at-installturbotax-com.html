How to Activate Your TurboTax at installturbotax.com



Activate Turbotax Download File, Go to www.InstallTurboTax.com, sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit license code, click Continue to activate, download and install TurboTax if needed, then launch the software and start filing your taxes.

Key Highlights: Steps to Activate TurboTax at InstallTurboTax.com

  1. Visit the Official Website – Go to www.InstallTurboTax.com on your web browser.
  2. Sign in to Your Intuit Account –Use your existing Intuit credentials or create a new account if you don’t have one.
  3. Enter Your License Code –Locate and Enter your 16-digit TurboTax license code from your purchase confirmation email or product box and input it in the activation field.
  4. Click “Continue” –This will validate your code and grant access to the software.
  5. Download TurboTax – If you haven’t already installed it, select the version 2024 or 2025 you purchased and click the download button.
  6. Install the Software – Open the downloaded setup file and follow the on-screen installation instructions.
  7. Launch TurboTax – Once installed, open TurboTax and sign in to your Intuit account again for final verification.
  8. Begin Your Tax Return –Follow the step-by-step guide to start preparing and filing your taxes easily.

For assistance, visit the TurboTax Help Center.
