How to installturbotax.com



InstallTurboTax.com is the trusted platform for downloading and installing TurboTax with a valid license code. Log in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit code, and select the correct version to download. Follow the step-by-step installation guide to set up the software on your computer. Once installed, open TurboTax, activate it and begin your tax preparation seamlessly. 

How do I Installturbotax.com?

  1. Go to InstallTurboTax.com.
  2. Log in with your Intuit account or create a new one.
  3. Enter your 16-digit TurboTax license code and click "Continue."
  4. Select the correct TurboTax version for your purchase.
  5. Click the "Download" button to save the installation file.
  6. Find the file in your computer’s download folder.
  7. Double-click the file and follow the on-screen installation instructions.
  8. Agree to the terms and conditions to proceed.
  9. Wait for the installation to finish and click “Finish.”
  10. Open the software, sign in, and start preparing your taxes.

Installturbotax.com from CD/DVD

  1. Prepare Your System: Close all running applications and temporarily disable any antivirus or firewall software to prevent installation issues.
  2. Insert the CD/DVD: Place the TurboTax CD into your optical drive.
  3. Start Installation:
    1. If the installation does not start automatically, press Windows + R to open the Run dialog, type D:\setup.exe (replace D with your CD drive letter), and press Enter.
    2. Follow the prompts on the screen.
  4. Activate TurboTax:
    1. After installation, open TurboTax and sign in to your Intuit Account.
    2. Enter your license code when prompted to complete activation.

Additional Notes

  1. Ensure that your computer meets the system requirements for the version of TurboTax you are installing.
  2. If you encounter issues during installation or activation, consult TurboTax support for assistance
