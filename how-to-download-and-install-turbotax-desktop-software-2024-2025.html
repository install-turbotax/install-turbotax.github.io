How to Download and Install TurboTax Desktop Software 2024-2025

  3. Enter Your 16-Digit License Code
    • Find your 16-digit license code from your purchase email or the packaging. Enter the code in the designated field on the website. This step is crucial for activating your TurboTax software.
  4. Click "Continue" to Validate
    • Once you’ve entered the code, click Continue. The system will verify the license code, ensuring you have the right to download and use the software.

    • After validating your license, you’ll be prompted to select the version of TurboTax you’ve purchased (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, Home & Business). Click the Download button to start the process.

    • After the download is complete, open the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. This will guide you through the installation process and ensure the software is correctly set up on your computer.
  7. Activate the Software
    • Once installed, open TurboTax Desktop 2024-2025, and sign in to your Intuit account. This step will finalize the activation process and allow you to begin working on your tax return.
  8. Start Your Tax Return
    • With the software installed and activated, you can now start entering your tax details. TurboTax will guide you through each step, helping you claim deductions, credits, and complete your tax filing.
  9. Access Your Return Anytime
    • Your return is saved to your Intuit account, allowing you to access and edit it anytime. Make sure to save your work frequently for your records.
  10. File Your Taxes
