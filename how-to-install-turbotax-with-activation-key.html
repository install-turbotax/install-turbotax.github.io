How do I install TurboTax with activation key?



Go to InstallTurboTax.com to download TurboTax 2023., sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit activation key, download and install the software, and follow the easy steps to start filing your taxes.

Key Highlights: Steps to Install TurboTax with an Activation Key

  1. Go to the Website – Visit InstallTurboTax.com to download TurboTax 2023..
  2. Sign In or Create an Account –Log in to your Intuit account or sign up if you're new.
  3. Enter Your Activation Key –Type the 16-digit activation key from your purchase.
  4. Download TurboTax –Select your purchased version and click the download button.
  5. Install the Software – Open the downloaded file and follow the simple instructions on the screen.
  6. Launch TurboTax – After installation, open TurboTax and sign in to your account again.
  7. Start Filing Your Taxes – Follow the step-by-step guide to prepare and file your tax return easily.
