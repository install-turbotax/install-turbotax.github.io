How to Install TurboTax 2024 with a License Code: Step-by-Step Guide



To install TurboTax 2024 with license code, Visit www.installturbotax.com, sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit license code for activation, download and install TurboTax 2024, then launch the software and start preparing your tax return with step-by-step guidance.

Step-by-Step Guide: Install TurboTax 2024 with a License Code

  1. Visit the Official TurboTax Website - Open your web browser and go to www.installturbotax.com. This is the official platform to download and activate your software.
  2. Log In or Create an Intuit Account - Use your existing Intuit credentials or set up a new account to proceed with the installation.
  3. Enter Your License Code – Find your 16-digit TurboTax license code from your email confirmation or product packaging and enter it in the designated field.
  4. Verify and Activate – Click Continue to validate your license and gain access to the software download.
  5. Download TurboTax 2024 – Select the version you purchased (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, or Home & Business) and click Download to save the setup file.
  6. Install the Software – Open the downloaded file, follow the step-by-step installation prompts, and complete the setup process.
  7. Launch and Sign In – Once installed, open TurboTax, sign in to your Intuit account again for final activation.
  8. Start Your Tax Return – Begin entering your tax details with TurboTax’s user-friendly interface and guided instructions.

For any issues or troubleshooting, visit the TurboTax Help Center.
