What are the Steps to Download TurboTax with license code



To download TurboTax using your license code, go to www.Installturbotax.com , sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit license code, select the version you purchased, and click Download. Once the download is complete, install the software and start your tax return with step-by-step guidance.

Key Highlights: Download TurboTax with License Code

  1. Go to Installturbotax.com – Visit the official website to get started on downloading TurboTax with your license code.
  2. Sign In or Create an Intuit Account – Log in with your existing Intuit account or create a new one to proceed.
  3. Enter Your 16-Digit License Code – Locate your license code from your purchase email or product packaging and enter it on the website to activate your TurboTax software.
  4. Click "Continue" to Verify – Once your code is entered, click Continue to verify it and move forward with the download process.
  5. Download TurboTax with License Code – Choose the version of TurboTax you purchased (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, or Home & Business), and click Download to get the installation file.
  6. Run the Installer – After downloading, open the installation file and follow the on-screen instructions to install TurboTax on your device.
  7. Launch TurboTax and Sign In – Once installed, open TurboTax, sign in again to complete activation, and ensure everything is set up correctly.
  8. Start Your Tax Return – Follow the easy, step-by-step guidance provided by TurboTax to begin entering your tax details and file your return.
