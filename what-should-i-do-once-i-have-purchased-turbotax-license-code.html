What should I do once I have purchased a TurboTax license code



To Use Your Purchased TurboTax License Code, Go to installturbotax.com, sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit license code for activation, download and install TurboTax, then launch the software and start your tax return with guided assistance.

Key Highlights: How to Use Your TurboTax License Code

  1. Go to the Official TurboTax Website – Visit installturbotax.com to begin the activation process.
  2. Sign In to Your Intuit Account – Use your existing Intuit account credentials or create a new account if you don’t have one.
  3. Enter Your 16-Digit License Code – Locate the license code from your purchase confirmation email or product packaging and input it in the designated field.
  4. Click "Continue" to Validate – The system will verify your license code and unlock access to the software download.
  5. Download TurboTax – Choose your purchased version (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, Home & Business) and click Download to save the installation file.
  6. Install TurboTax on Your Computer – Open the downloaded file, follow the on-screen installation instructions, and complete the setup.
  7. Launch TurboTax and Sign In Again – Open TurboTax after installation and log in to finalize activation.
  8. Start Your Tax Return – Begin entering your tax information with TurboTax’s step-by-step guidance.

For any questions or help, you can visit the TurboTax Help Center.
