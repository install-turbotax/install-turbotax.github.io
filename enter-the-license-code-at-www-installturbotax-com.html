Visit www.Installturbotax.com, sign in to your Intuit account, enter your 16-digit license code, validate, download and install TurboTax, then start your tax return with guided steps.

Key Highlights: How to Enter Your License Code at www.InstallTurboTax.com

  1. Visit the Official Website – Go to www.Installturbotax.com in your web browser.
  2. Sign In to Your Intuit Account – Use your existing Intuit login or create a new account if needed.
  3. Enter Your 16-Digit License Code – Locate your code from your purchase confirmation email or product box and input it in the required field.
  4. Click "Continue" to Validate – TurboTax will verify your license code before proceeding.
  5. Download TurboTax – Select your purchased version and click Download to get the installation file.
  6. Install and Activate – Open the downloaded file, follow the on-screen instructions to install TurboTax, and sign in again to complete activation.
  7. Start Your Tax Return – Once activated, begin entering your tax details using TurboTax’s step-by-step guidance.

For any issues, visit the TurboTax Help Center.
