How do i Download TurboTax 2024 that i have already purchased?



To Download TurboTax 2024 you have Already Purchased, Go to www.InstallTurboTax.com, sign in to your Intuit account, navigate to "Downloads" or "My Products," select TurboTax 2024, click Download, install the software, and start filing your taxes.

Key Highlights: How to Download TurboTax 2024 Already Purchased

  1. Go to www.InstallTurboTax.com.
  2. Sign in to your Intuit account used for the purchase.
  3. Go to "Downloads" or "My Products" in your account.
  4. Select TurboTax 2024 from your purchased products.
  5. Click the "Download" button and save the file.
  6. Run the installer and follow the on-screen steps.
  7. Launch TurboTax and start your tax return.
