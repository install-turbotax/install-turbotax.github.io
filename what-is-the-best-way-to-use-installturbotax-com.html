Key Highlights: How do i use installturbotax.com

  1. Go to the Official Website – Visit www.installturbotax.com on your browser to start the installation process.
  2. Sign In to Your Intuit Account – Log in with your existing account, or create a new account if you don’t already have one.
  3. Enter Your License Code – Enter the 16-digit license code found in your purchase confirmation email or product packaging.
  4. Download TurboTax – Choose your purchased version (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, Home & Business) and click the Download button to begin the installation file download.
  5. Run the Installer – Once the download is complete, open the installation file and follow the on-screen instructions to install TurboTax on your computer.
  6. Activate TurboTax – Open the software and sign in to complete the activation process.
  7. Start Your Tax Return – Begin using TurboTax to enter your tax information and file your return with the step-by-step guidance provided.
