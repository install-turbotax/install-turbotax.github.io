How Do You Install TurboTax 2024?



Install TurboTax 2024 from InstallTurboTax.com, enter your 16-digit license code, sign in to your Intuit account, and follow the simple setup process to start filing your taxes with ease.

Key Highlights: Installing TurboTax 2024

  1. Visit the Official Website –Go to InstallTurboTax.com to begin the installation process.
  2. Sign in to Your Intuit Account –Log in with your existing credentials or create a new account if needed.
  3. Download TurboTax 2024 –Select your purchased version (Basic, Deluxe, Premier, Home & Business, or Business) and download the installer.
  4. Run the Installation –Open the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions to install TurboTax 2024.
  5. Enter Your License Code –When prompted, input the 16-character license code (XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) to activate your software.
  6. Start Filing Your Taxes –Follow the step-by-step guidance to complete your 2024 tax return easily and accurately.
